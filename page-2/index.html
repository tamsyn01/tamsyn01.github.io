Black Saturday Newspaper Cover


The physical presence of language, and the ways in which we have learnt to interact with the written word makes for an indescribable complication. Translation of the effect and charisma that sets a word to its meaning is hard, and often lost. For this Newspaper, its purpose is that of an informant; language however throught the eyes of our present translation applications transforms this object into an analysis of its form, focusing in on the positioning and scale of the title, so much so it misses smaller print on the top fold of the paper, as well as within the imagery. Within this object translation apps are found to be flawed in their scanning of an image and hence the entirety of their provided context.
